01 — PROCESS

Six simple steps.
Zero hassle.

From typing your list to knocking on your door — here's exactly how Knock Knock gets your favorite things to you, fast and verified.

STEP 01

Choose or enter the store

Pick any supermarket, bakery, pharmacy, or restaurant. We'll go exactly where you tell us.

STEP 02

List the items you need

Type your shopping list in plain words. Attach a reference image if you have one.

STEP 03

Enter delivery address

Drop your pin or type your address. We auto-calculate the delivery fee from distance.

STEP 04

Pay 50% in advance

Secure your slot with half the estimated total via Stripe or bank transfer. Fully encrypted.

STEP 05

Knock Knock picks up

Our verified rider arrives at the store, collects your items, and starts the live tracker.

STEP 06

Pay the remaining 50% COD

Settle the balance in cash when your order arrives. Inspect, then pay. Simple.

ABOUT US

We don't sell products.
We run errands.

Knock Knock started with a simple frustration: getting things from the stores we already loved was harder than it should be. Delivery apps force you to pick from their catalog. We wanted a service that goes to our bakery, our pharmacist, our butcher.

So we built one. Knock Knock is not a marketplace. We don't stock products, we don't sell inventory, and we don't take a cut from stores. We're a pure pickup-and-delivery service — you tell us where, you tell us what, and our riders do the rest.

Today we operate in Sargodha with verified riders, completing deliveries every month. We've kept the same rule since day one: inspect before you pay. If something's not right at your door, you don't pay for it.

LEGAL

Privacy Policy

Knock Knock Delivery Service ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

1. Information we collect

Name, phone number, email, delivery addresses, order history, and payment metadata. We do not store full card numbers — payments are processed by Stripe.

2. How we use it

To process deliveries, assign riders, calculate fees, send order updates, and improve our routes. We never sell your data.

LEGAL

Terms & Conditions

By using Knock Knock, you agree to the following terms.

1. Service scope

Knock Knock is a pickup and delivery service only. We do not sell, stock, or inventory any products. We act as your agent to pick up items you have requested from third-party stores.

2. Payment

50% advance payment is required to confirm your order. The remaining 50% is payable in cash on delivery. If items are unavailable at the store, the advance is refunded within 3 business days.
